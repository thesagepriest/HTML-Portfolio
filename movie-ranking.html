My Top 5 Animes

  • The Naruto Series

    • Pretty much one of the most popular animes of all time and one of the big three of anime, The Naruto series follows the story of a young, baseless outcast shinobi with a dream of becoming hokage "the strongest shinobi ever". This is a story of how much hard work pays off and how much the opinion of people about a person can chnage with regards to their growth. This is probably my favourite anime of all time.

  • The Promised Neverland

    • It follows the story of three orphaned children "Emma, Ray and Norman" and their siblings and how they try to escape from thier home/prison. If you like plot twists and psychologically draining movies, then this is a must watch for you.

  • Solo Levelling

    • If you like from zero to hero troupes, then you should definitely see this one. The best anime of 2024, it follows the story of Sun Jinwoo a young korean F-class hunter who becomes the strongest hunter in the world following a near death experience.

  • Kaiju No 8

    • What happens when you become the very monster you hate and hunt? Find out in this intriguing story of middle aged young man Kafka Hibino. From being a failure who couldn't get into the Anti Kaiju Defense Force to becoming a Kaiju and passing the AKDF exams, watch Kafka become an old man in a field of young people to eventually saving the world. If you believe in chasing your dreams no matter what age you are regardless of what people think or say, then you join in the cheer for Kafka Hibino
